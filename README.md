Bookmarked.

This is an e-commerce student website project which focuses on providing the reading community a platform to buy and sell e-books to each other. Users can upload books for listing, modify their listings, and view the books they are listing and other users' listings in the books displayed in the digital storefront and via search. Searching can be done through manually typing for the book's title or through genre-based filters. Users are given pages to store books. For books unbought but among their target purchases, they can store them in their wishlist. Books can also be removed from the wishlist. For books that they bought, they are stored in their library, where they can also apply genre-filtering to view their books in a more organized way by their genre. In their library, they can navigate to collections, which is a feature that allows them to group select books in custom-named folders called collection. When they buy books, they can also engage with the community by leaving reviews, which is visible to the seller and other users. They can also manage these collections as they can add more or remove them. Books inside collections can also be changed or removed. Transparency is also implemented in this website by allowing users to track their purchase history and sales/transaction history.

Overall, this website was built to cater to book lovers and allow the target users a platform to expand their literary sources, discover new titles to get, or even share and profit from their books.
